# CalTrack Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-03-13

This Privacy Policy explains how CalTrack ("**we**", "**us**", "**our**") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the CalTrack mobile application (the "**App**").

## 1) Summary (plain English)
- CalTrack is **local-first** for v1: your entries are stored on your device.
- The App may query **public food databases** to look up nutrition information.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## 2) Information you provide
You may enter information such as:
- Meal names, notes, serving counts, and nutrition values.

## 3) Information collected automatically
We do not intentionally collect identifying analytics in v1.

Your device/OS may generate standard diagnostics (e.g., crash logs) depending on platform settings.

## 4) How we use information
We use information to:
- Store and display your nutrition entries.
- Calculate totals, trends, and insights.
- Provide reminders if you enable notifications.

## 5) Third-party services / API calls
The App may contact third-party services to look up nutrition data (for example, barcode or text search).

When you use these features, your device may send:
- Your search text or barcode value
- Basic request metadata (e.g., IP address) to the third-party provider

Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

## 6) Notifications
If you enable reminders, the App schedules **local notifications** on your device. We do not receive notification delivery data in v1.

## 7) Data retention
Your entries are stored on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.

## 8) Your choices
You can:
- Edit or delete entries in the App.
- Disable reminders in Settings.

## 9) Children
The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us.

## 10) Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date will be updated.
